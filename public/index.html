DPDPA Compliance Assessment Tool

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), is expected to revamp how organizations collect, use, store, and manage personal data in India. For many businesses, one of the most difficult questions is where to begin.

Trilegal's DPDPA Compliance Assessment Tool is designed to help you take that first step. With 3-5 short answers, you will receive a customized, high-level compliance roadmap to help you identify the nature and likely extent of your obligations under the DPDPA.

This roadmap is useful for organizations at all stages of their compliance journey and helps assess the potential impact of the DPDPA on their operations or provides a practical starting point for internal discussions.
